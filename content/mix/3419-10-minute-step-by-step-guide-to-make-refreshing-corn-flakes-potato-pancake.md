##### The ingredients needed to cook Corn Flakes Potato Pancake:

1. Get 2 medium sized boiled potato
1. Use 1 cup corn flakes
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp garam masala
1. Use 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder
1. Provide 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
1. Provide 1/2 tsp chaat masala powder
1. You need  Oil for frying
1. Prepare  Tomato sauce for garnishing


##### Instructions to make Corn Flakes Potato Pancake:

1. In a bowl take all ingredients and mix it well altogether.
1. Heat a pan and pour 2 tsp oil and spread it nicely.
1. Take little bit mixture of lemon sized balls from potato mixture,slightly flatten it with the palm and put on pan.
1. Now add some corn flakes over potato pancakes and press it with spatula,flip the pan cake,add 1 tsp oil more and fry from another side also until it becomes golden and crispy.
1. Garnish with tomato sauce and serve hot,Corn Flakes Potato Pancakes is ready.
