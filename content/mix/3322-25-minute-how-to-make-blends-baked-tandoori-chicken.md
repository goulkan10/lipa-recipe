##### The ingredients needed to cook Baked Tandoori Chicken:

1. Take 1/2 kg Chicken drumstick
1. Use 1 Packet Shan Tandoori Masala
1. You need 1/2 cup Yogurt
1. You need  Butter to brush on chicken


##### Instructions to make Baked Tandoori Chicken:

1. Marinate the chicken with Shan Tandoori masala and yogurt. Marinate it for 1 hour.
1. Preheat the oven. Set the drumstick on the baking tray.
1. Bake it for 45 minute.
1. In the end brush some melted butter on the chicken. Serve it with fries.
