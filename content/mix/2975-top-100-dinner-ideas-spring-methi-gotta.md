##### The ingredients needed to make Methi gotta:

1. Use 2 cup chopped methi (fenugreek)leaves
1. Use 1/2 cup coriander leaves
1. Get 11/2 cup coarse besan(gram flour)(mota besan)
1. Get 2-3 Chopped green chillies
1. You need 1 tsp sugar
1. Take 2 tsp haldi(Turmeric) powder
1. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
1. Get 1 tbsp dahi/curd
1. Get 1 tbsp lemon juice
1. You need 1 tsp cooking soda
1. Get to taste Salt
1. Use as needed Oil for frying


##### Instructions to make Methi gotta:

1. In one pot put chopped methi leaves add coriander leaves,besan,green chillies red chilli powder,dahi, lemon juice, salt,soda mix add little water and make thick batter.heat oil in kadai. Take small portion put in oil and fry golden brown at medium heat. Serve.
