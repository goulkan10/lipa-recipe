1. Get 1/2 tsp salt
1. Take 1 tsp sugar
1. Provide 1/2 tsp pepper
1. Provide 1/2 tsp sugar for top on bread
1. Use 1 bunch coriander use both roots and leaves
1. You need 2 clove garlic
1. Get 1 red chili,deseeded,thin sliced for decoration
