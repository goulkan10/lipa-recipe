##### The ingredients needed to prepare Scrumptious Fried ‘Ikan Bilis’ &amp; Walnuts:

1. You need  ‘Ikan Bilis’ (Dried Anchovy Fillets)
1. You need  (about 1 cupful) Walnuts
1. You need  Canola Oil
1. Take  Sugar
1. Take  Sake (Rice wine)
1. Take  Soy Sauce
1. Use  Rice Vinegar
1. Take  Toasted Sesame Seeds


##### Steps to make Scrumptious Fried ‘Ikan Bilis’ &amp; Walnuts:

1. Wash ‘Ikan Bilis’ with cold water a couple of times and clean them. Remove unwanted pieces such as scales and heads. Drain well. *This process softens very dry ‘Ikan Bilis’ and also reduces the saltiness.
1. In a small bowl, combine Sugar, Soy Sauce and Sake (Rice Wine).
1. Heat Oil in a frying pan over low heat, cook ‘Ikan Bilis’ and Walnuts until golden and crunchy, but do not over cook.
1. Add the sauce mixture, stir to combine, and remove from heat. Sprinkle Rice Vinegar over and stir. The sauce will thicken with the remaining heat. Sprinkle with Toasted Sesame Seeds.
