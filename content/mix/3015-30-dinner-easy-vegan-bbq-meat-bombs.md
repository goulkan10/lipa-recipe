##### The ingredients needed to cook BBQ Meat bombs:

1. Take 1 packages Ground turkey
1. You need 1/4 cup Oatmeal
1. Use 1 packages meatloaf seasoning (or whatever meat seasoning you usually use)
1. Take 1 egg
1. Use 1 large yellow onion
1. Take 1 lb bacon
1. Prepare 1/2 cup BBQ sauce


##### Steps to make BBQ Meat bombs:

1. Mix together ground turkey with oatmeal, egg, &amp; seasoning.
1. Cut the top &amp; bottom of the onion then cut it in half.  Separate the onion into layers.
1. Scoop out the meat into balls and place each ball onto an onion layer then wrap with a strip of bacon.
1. Bake at 425 for 40 minutes in a baking dish.  Then remove &amp; brush with BBQ sauce, return to oven &amp; bake for 5 minutes more.
