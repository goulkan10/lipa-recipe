1. Get 1 Swiss cheese block or pre sliced swiss cheese
1. Use 1/2 lb. Deli sliced cornedbeef
1. Prepare 1 (14 oz.) can Silver Floss shredded sauerkraut
1. Use 1 jar Thousand Island dressing
