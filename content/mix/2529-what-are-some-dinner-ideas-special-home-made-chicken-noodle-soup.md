1. Get 1 tbsp basil
1. You need 1 tbsp parsly
1. Use 1 tbsp oragano
1. Prepare 1 tsp ground pepper
1. Provide 4 cup water
1. Take 1/2 lb essenhaus extra wide homestyle noodles
