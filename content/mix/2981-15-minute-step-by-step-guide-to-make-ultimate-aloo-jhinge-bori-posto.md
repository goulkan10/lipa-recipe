##### The ingredients needed to prepare Aloo Jhinge Bori Posto:

1. Provide 2 Potato ,  cut   into   cubes ,
1. Get 1 onion Medium size    , chopped ,
1. Use 4 Green chilli ,  slitted ,
1. Prepare 1 tsp Kalonji (Kalo Jeera)
1. Use 3 tbsps Mustard oil ,
1. Get 1   1/2 cups Poppy seed green chilli salt ,  grounded   to   a   fine   paste   with      and    ,
1. Use 8 Bori ,  fried   lightly ,

##### Steps to make Aloo Jhinge Bori Posto:

1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a Kadai (a round bottomed pan). Fry the potatoes and Jhinge and keep them aside.
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in pan. To it add Kalonji and 2 green chillies. Once it starts to crackle, add onion and a pinch of salt and let it cook a little.
1. To this add the fried potatoes and jhinge. Add salt, sugar and turmeric. Cover and saute alternatively for some time.
1. Sprinkle little water to cook.
1. Once the vegetables are tender, add the fried Boris and poppy paste to it.
1. Mix thoroughly.
1. Sprinkle 1 tbsp mustard oil from above and put 2-3 slitted green chillies.
1. Keep this covered for 5-10 mins before serving.
