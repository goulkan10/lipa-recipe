##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Meatball, Mushroom pasta:

1. Provide  Meatballs
1. You need 1 pound ground beef 80/20
1. You need 1 1/4 cup cooked rice
1. Take 2 teaspoons garlic powder
1. Use 2 teaspoons onion powder
1. Use 1 package mushroom gravy, powdered
1. You need 3 tablespoons bread crumbs
1. Prepare 1 egg
1. You need 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1. You need to taste salt
1. Take  Sauce
1. You need 1 can cream of mushroom soup
1. Take 1 can milk
1. Get 1 black pepper to taste


##### Instructions to make Easy Meatball, Mushroom pasta:

1. Mix all Meatball ingredients. I was using left over rice, so declump it if you do also.
1. I made this the night before, and let it sit until the next day, in the fridge.
1. Make meatballs, we made ours about golf ball sized.
1. Brown in a little oil in a skillet large enough. I used medium high heat.
1. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
1. After meatballs are browned, add sauce.
1. Once sauce starts to bubble, turn heat down to medium low.
1. Cook until meatballs are cooked through. For me it was about 10 minutes. It depends on the size of your meatballs, and how long you brown them.
1. I served ours over a mix of noodles.
1. Garnish with green onions, parsley, cheese or nothing.
